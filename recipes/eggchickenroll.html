Recipe for Egg Chicken Roll

Description

Egg Chicken Roll is a popular Indian street food where a soft, flaky paratha is layered with a cooked egg and stuffed with spicy, juicy chicken pieces. The chicken is usually sautéed with onions, peppers, and flavorful spices, while the egg forms a thin, crispy layer that wraps around the bread, adding richness and texture.

Rolled up tightly and often garnished with onions, green chilies, and a dash of tangy sauces, Egg Chicken Roll is loved for its delicious mix of flavors and easy, on-the-go style. It’s a perfect quick meal that blends protein, spice, and a satisfying crunch, making it a global favorite for anyone craving a taste of Indian street flavors.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken pieces
  • Paratha or flatbread
  • Eggs
  • Onion
  • Green bell pepper
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Green chilies (optional)
  • Tomato ketchup or mint chutney (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook chicken with onions, bell peppers, spices, and salt.
  2. Scramble or fry an egg, and cook it into a thin omelette.
  3. Heat paratha and layer with the cooked egg.
  4. Add the cooked chicken mixture on top.
  5. Roll tightly and serve with chutney or sauce.
